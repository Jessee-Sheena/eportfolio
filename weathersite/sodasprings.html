By: Staff Writer

Posted: Oct 16, 2018 12:05 PM MDT

The National Weather Service has issued special weather alerts warning the public about a series of fast-moving snowstorms that are forecast to hit East Idaho starting Thursday morning and continuing through Sunday. East Idaho’s mountains are forecast to receive the most snow from the storms. Over 15 inches of snow could fall Thursday morning through Sunday in East Idaho’s higher elevations, making for hazardous driving conditions in those areas. The weather service says anyone wanting to travel anywhere in East Idaho over the extended holiday weekend should check the forecast and local conditions and “plan accordingly” before taking to the road.
